About Us


Ken MacLaughlin

Director, Producer, and Writer

This is Ken's bio. He is a man of many talents and mystery. Until he tells us more about himself. Here's some more generic text to test to proportions I've made.

Blake Studwell

Director of Photography

Cursed with the highest documented IQ of any living being, Blake spent his entire childhood being studied in laboratories throughout eastern Europe. Legend has it that he never sleeps and is immune to electricity. If fed after midnight, he transforms into a cocoon and mutates into a deadly reptilian creature. His favorite restaurant is Chili's.

Dominick Racano

Asst. Director, Producer, and Writer

Dom is the creator of the elaborate sketches. We walk into the writers room with his 1-2 pagers, only to find out there's like 30 cast members, glitter, a high school marching band juggling chainsaws, and it all takes place inside a Fiat.

Lee Ogilvie

Producer, Writer, and Web Developer

Born with an oversized mouth and a penchant for irreverence, Lee has lived most of her life spitting out her foot. Always quick with a quip at a funeral or when being pulled over, she lives completely out of context. She is the last millennial to have made an actual mix tape.

Jason Kim

Producer and Writer

Known to us as "The Emperor," he rules us all with an iron fist. A self-proclaimed sex addict, he also has a dog grooming business. The two are definitely mutually exclusive.
